· About me

My PhD was carried out as a Marie Skłodowska-Curie ENLIGHTEN fellow in the group of Prof. Maciej Lewenstein, co-supervised by Dr. Marcin Płodzień, exploring how many-body spin systems can be simulated and engineered to reach the ultimate limits of precision measurement.

My work sits at the intersection of quantum simulation, quantum metrology, and machine learning — tensor networks, semidefinite programming, and variational circuit optimization.

Beyond research, I care about scientific outreach — co-founding the quantum awareness hub QTPi, and writing for children in Frontiers for Young Minds.
