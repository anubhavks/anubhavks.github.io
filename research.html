· Research & publications

How far can we push measurement precision with quantum many-body systems — and how do we simulate, certify, and control them at scale?

01

Quantum metrology & sensing

Optimal precision measurement with many-body spin systems — quantum Fisher information, thresholded field sensing, and topological quantum thermometry.

02

Quantum simulation of spin systems

Large-scale classical and variational simulation of many-body dynamics — tensor networks, frustrated geometries, and Hamiltonian engineering.

03

Certification & convex optimization

Semidefinite-programming approaches to entanglement detection and device-independent certification of metrological resources from measured data.

04

Machine learning for quantum systems

Attention-based neural networks for resource-efficient state tomography, classical shadows for metrology, and ML tools for state certification.
